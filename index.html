Cauliflower Mac-N-Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups elbow macaroni (or any shape)
  • ½ cup cauliflower florets
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ package cream cheese (8 ounce)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Kitchen Equipment

  • Stove
  • Large pot
  • Strainer
  • Wooden spoon
  • Steamer insert (or substitute with strainer)
  • Medium and large saucepan
  • Blender

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
  4. Bring water to a boil. Add cauliflower, cover, and steam until tender, 5 to 6 minutes.
  5. Place cauliflower in a blender or food processor; blend until smooth.
  6. Coat a large saucepan with non-stick cooking spray; place over medium heat.
  7. Add oil and flour; cook, stirring constantly, until a thick paste forms, 1 to 2 minutes.
  8. Add milk; cook and stir until mixture thickens, 3 to 4 minutes.
  9. Mix cauliflower, Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, salt, and pepper into milk mixture; stir until sauce is smooth.
  10. Fold macaroni into sauce and serve!
